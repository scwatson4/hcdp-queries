Hurricane Lane is clearly visible in the historical monthly data. The highest monthly total for August 2018 was 2,166mm (85.3 in) at Big Island windward station 140.5. Subtracting normal August rainfall (~475mm), roughly 1,700mm (~67 in) is attributable to Lane, though this is a monthly proxy — published daily-resolution storm totals report 50-58 inches over August 22-26 specifically. The slight overestimate is expected from using monthly data, since we cannot isolate the 4-day storm window from the full month. August 2018 was a dramatic outlier: its network-wide average (478mm) was 4x a typical August (~120mm), and 23 stations exceeded 1,500mm for the month.
